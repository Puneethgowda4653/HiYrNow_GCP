How HiYrNow works — AI that helps you hire

A simple, repeatable flow that turns inputs (JDs, CVs, signals) into prioritized actions and outcomes.

1

Ingest & normalize

We ingest JDs, resumes, referrals, and public profiles — then normalise skills, roles and signals into a single talent graph.

2

Analyze & score

Our models produce skill maps, intent signals, fit scores and gap analysis — all with explainable factors you can inspect.

3

Generate & recommend

Auto-generate JDs, interview plans and candidate briefings; produce ranked shortlists and recommended next steps for both recruiters and candidates.

4

Act & integrate

Push shortlists to your ATS, send candidate nudges via Slack/email, and export stakeholder-ready briefs — all auditable and traceable.

5

Measure & iterate

Every action feeds the talent graph — we measure shortlist conversion, interview drift and offer friction, then iterate models and guardrails.

All stages include human-in-the-loop checks, bias guardrails, and explainability so hiring teams retain control while the AI removes friction.
